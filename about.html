The Self-Upliftment Foundation

Our vision is underpinned by the aspiration to invest in and uplift the lives of every human being we work with.

We are motivated by love for the human spirit, to be self-reliant and to break free of circumstances that limit. Our main objective is to ensure that each individual who is willing to learn and are committed to apply life-changing knowledge, enjoys successful provident living.

  • Financially
  • Emotionally
  • Socially

Ours is the responsibility to paint a bright future for anyone who dares to work at a realistic plan.

Our Values

We hold dear to heart values that assist use in achieving our mission and vision.

Motivation

Our only motivation is love. Love for people. The love to see another succeed at becoming self-reliant and taking care of their needs and responsibilities.

People

We believe that everything good in life revolves around people with solid values. As we strive to uphold the value that people are the greatest assets in our organization, we endeavour to invest everything that is good and wholesome in our people.

Accountabilty

We expect to be held accountable and pride ourselves on the responsibility we carry to provide reckoning.
