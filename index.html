    Our Mission

    Our purpose is to inspire, educate and elevate the individuals we serve into the realms of self-reliance and a productive life, while remaining true to the principles of dignity and agency to choose. We are committed to return beauty for ashes and building the foundation on a solid base of transformational servant-leaders whose only aim it is to help prepare the world for better things to come.

    Health

    We strive to assist with medical assistance to those who are not able to fund themseves.

    Education

    Knowledge is the key to success. We strive to achieve by providing the communities with education.

    Self-Reliance

    Self-Reliance is the pillar that strengthens the community and church at large.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is underpinned by the aspiration to invest in and uplift the lives of every human being we work with. We are motivated by love for the human spirit, to be self-reliant and to break free of circumstances that limit. Our main objective is to ensure that each individual who is willing to learn and are committed to apply life-changing knowledge, enjoys successful provident living. Ours is the responsibility to paint a bright future for anyone who dares to work at a realistic plan, mapping out a journey to becoming mature; financially, emotionally, and socially.
